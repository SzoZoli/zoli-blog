## Szombathy Zoltán
**PhD Candidate in Economics · Applied AI Analyst**

Doctoral researcher at the University of Pécs (PTE), Faculty of Business and Economics, focused on regional economic changes in Vojvodina (Serbia) under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Kaposi Zoltán DSc. My research sits at the intersection of regional economics, agricultural transformation, and the application of artificial intelligence to economic analysis.

Beyond academic research, I build AI-powered platforms for regulatory compliance and market intelligence. [miaudit.hu](https://miaudit.hu) helps organisations assess their readiness for the EU AI Act. [LifeAIPro](https://lifeaipro.com) is a multi-agent advisory platform serving users across three languages. I also publish independent market research on how AI and PropTech are reshaping real estate and investment flows across Central and Eastern Europe and the Western Balkans.

### Coverage areas

**AI & Regulatory Compliance** — EU AI Act implementation, Fundamental Rights Impact Assessments (FRIA), GDPR/DORA/NIS2 intersections, automated compliance audit platforms, multi-agent document review systems.

**CEE & Western Balkans Market Intelligence** — Data-driven analysis of property markets, PropTech ecosystems, tourism real estate, and residential pricing. Focus on how transparency improvements, cadastral digitalisation, and EU accession reshape investment flows.

**Applied AI & LLM Engineering** — RAG systems, multi-agent architectures, domain-specific knowledge bases, market sentiment analysis, and AI maturity assessment frameworks.

**Agricultural Economics & Regional Development** — Structural economic change in Vojvodina, land-use patterns, crop yield analysis, climate impact modelling, and AI applications in agricultural research.
